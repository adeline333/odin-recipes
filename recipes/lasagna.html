Lasagna

Description

This homemade lasagna is filled with Italian sausage, ground beef, and three kinds of cheese. The perfect meal for a family gathering or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Italian seasoning, basil, salt, fennel seeds, black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce by browning sausage and ground beef with onion and garlic.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Simmer with spices.
  3. Cook the lasagna noodles until tender.
  4. Mix ricotta with egg, parsley, and some cheese.
  5. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta mixture, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  6. Cover with foil and bake.
  7. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
