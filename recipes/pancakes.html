Fluffy Maple Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

Light, fluffy pancakes with a hint of maple syrup, perfect for breakfast or brunch. These pancakes cook up golden and delicious every time!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, eggs, maple syrup, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and coat with cooking spray.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter onto skillet; cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Repeat with remaining batter.
