Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Sandwiches

Description

Perfect for game day, these Buffalo chicken sandwiches are slow-cooked to tender perfection with a spicy kick and served on hoagie rolls with ranch or blue cheese dressing.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (17.5 oz) bottle Buffalo wing sauce, divided
  • 1/2 (1 oz) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in a slow cooker and add 3/4 of the wing sauce and ranch mix.
  2. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.
  3. Shred the chicken in the slow cooker with two forks. Stir in butter.
  4. Serve shredded chicken on hoagie rolls with remaining Buffalo sauce on the side.
